## positive lookahead


```js
/is(?=\sthe)/
//kamal is the is developer
```

* [demo](https://regexr.com/3nt8t)

## negative lookahead


```js
/is(?!\sthe)/
//kamal is the is developer
```

* [demo](https://regexr.com/3nt90)
